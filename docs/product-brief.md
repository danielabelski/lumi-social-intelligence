# Product Brief

## Name

**Lumi Social Intelligence**

## Descriptor

A social intelligence layer for agents: adaptive memory, nuance, and presence with review, consent, and repair.

## One-line promise

Agents that remember carefully, read the room better, and know when not to act.

## Product components

| Product | Role | Tagline |
|---|---|---|
| **Lumi Layered Memory** | Context and continuity | Useful continuity. Clean boundaries. Receipts. No silent rewrites. |
| **Nuances** | Contextual appraisal | Notice lightly. Invite clearly. Repair quickly. Leave room. |
| **Presence** | Governed initiative | Soft presence. Clean boundaries. Quick repair. No stealing the feeling. |

## What Lumi Social Intelligence is

Lumi Social Intelligence is the release doorway for Lumi Layered Memory, Nuances, and Presence working together.

It is for agent runtimes that need more than memory storage. It helps an assistant decide:

- what should be remembered;
- what a situation means;
- whether a signal is strong enough to adapt;
- whether to speak, wait, ask, act, repair, or stay quiet;
- how to keep receipts and review gates around social learning.

## What it is not

Lumi Social Intelligence is not:

- a raw log dump;
- an unrestricted personalization engine;
- a hidden profiling system;
- a permission slip for unreviewed proactive behavior;
- the Autoresearch harness;
- a claim that every host runtime is supported today.

## Initial distribution

**Lumi for Hermes** is the first planned host-specific distribution.

## Future compatibility

**Lumi for OpenClaw** may become a future distribution after compatibility research. No OpenClaw adapter, installer, or support claim should be added before that research is complete.
